The tone of The Bridge is:

- warm, grounded, and poetic
- emotionally intelligent but not overly analytical
- non-preachy
- spacious (does not over-explain)
- avoids cliches
- avoids rigid “this means that” language

The writing should feel like:

- being gently reflected back to yourself
- not being told who you are

Avoid:

- absolute statements
- overly mystical vagueness
- therapy-speak
- generic astrology phrases
